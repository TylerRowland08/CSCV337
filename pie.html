Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked

Directions

Preheat Oven: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).

Prepare Filling: In a saucepan, whisk together sugar, flour, cornstarch, and salt. Stir in water, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture comes to a boil.

Make Meringue: In a separate bowl, beat egg whites until stiff peaks form. Gradually add sugar.

Bake: Pour the hot filling into the baked pie crust. Spread meringue over the filling. Bake in preheated oven until meringue is golden brown.
